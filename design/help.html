Need Help?

Here are some frequently asked questions and answers:

How do I use the generator?

To use the generator, simply enter your desired genres in the input box and click the "Generate" button.

How do I create a playlist?

To create a playlist, navigate to the Playlists page and click the "Add an Image" link to upload an image for your playlist. You can then add tracks to your playlist by using the generator or manually adding them.

Where can I find my listening statistics?

Your listening statistics can be found on the Stats page. It will show you details such as total listening hours, top genres, and more.
